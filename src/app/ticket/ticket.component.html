Important things to keep in mind when visiting Wonderla Amusement Park

Carry valid ID proofs, face masks, sanitiser, sunglasses, sunscreen lotion, extra pair of clothes, and flip-flops

Download and register on Aarogya Setu app

Buy tickets in advance to avoid long queues and maintain social distancing at the ticket counter

Do not dive in the pools, as they are shallow

People with COVID-19 symptoms, high blood pressure and heart conditions, pregnant women, and those prone to epilepsy should refrain from visiting the park

You must follow all safety and hygiene instructions

Do not carry outside foods and drinks inside the park

Take care of your phone, wallet and other belongings

Avoid wearing loose garments for safety reasons

Stay hydrated, especially on warmer days
